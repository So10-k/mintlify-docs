The tournament has its own **Discourse forum** at **discuss.miaswebsites.art** — for hot takes, week-by-week banter, and the **Tournament Talk** category. You don't need a separate account: the forum trusts the quiz site's sign-in via **DiscourseConnect** (formerly known as Discourse SSO), so signing in once on the quiz site is enough for both places.

<Note>
  You do need a tournament account first. Visit [/join](/joining) before clicking "Sign in" on the forum — DiscourseConnect can only mirror an identity that already exists on the quiz site.
</Note>

## How sign-in works

When you click **Sign in** on the forum:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discourse bounces you to quiz.miaswebsites.art">
    Discourse builds a signed payload and redirects you to `quiz.miaswebsites.art/api/discourse/sso`. The quiz site verifies Discourse's signature before doing anything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The quiz site checks your session">
    If you're already signed in (via MiaAuth or magic link), the quiz site uses that session immediately. If not, it sends you to /signin first — sign in there, then continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The quiz site signs a response and sends you back">
    Once your identity is confirmed, the quiz site builds a signed payload containing your email, display name, and avatar, and redirects you back to Discourse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discourse creates or updates your forum account">
    On first arrival, Discourse creates your forum account using the data the quiz site sent. On every subsequent sign-in, Discourse just updates your profile and logs you in. No welcome email, no separate verification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets shared with the forum

Each successful sign-in shares the following with Discourse:

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Email** | The address on your quiz account. Discourse uses it as the canonical identity. |
| **External ID** | Your `users.id` from the tournament — stable across name and email changes. |
| **Display name** | Your tournament name. If you rename yourself in the tournament, your forum name follows. |
| **Avatar** | The avatar you set on the quiz site (if any). |
| **Admin / moderator flag** | Auto-granted on the forum when your tournament `role === "author"`. Mia + Sam get full forum control with no extra setup. |
| **Group membership** | The tournament keeps Discourse groups like `players`, `semi_finalists`, and `finalists` in lockstep with the bracket. Each successful sign-in resyncs your group memberships. |

<Note>
  Discourse never sees a password because there isn't one. The forum trusts the quiz site's session entirely.
</Note>

## What you'll see

- **First time you sign in**: Discourse skips its "Welcome!" email — you already know what the site is — and lands you on the homepage with your tournament name and avatar.
- **Every time after**: The sign-in is invisible. You click **Sign in** on the forum, the page flashes, and you're signed in.

## Signing out

Signing out of Discourse only signs you out of the forum — your quiz-site session is unaffected. To sign out of both, sign out of the quiz site from its app menu; your forum session will end the next time it needs to check in.

## Forum categories worth bookmarking

- **Tournament Talk** — predictions, week-over-week recaps, hot takes about who's about to lose a heart.
- **Finals Room** — finalists-only category for the people still in it.
- Your eliminated friends can still post in the public categories — they just lose access to the finals one.
