## The full puzzle chain

This is the author-only walkthrough. It documents every step of the
SESSION 001 easter egg so the host (Sam) can verify the puzzle is
solvable, help anyone who gets stuck, and confirm screenshots that
get texted in.

**Final access code: `SUNSHINE`** (8 letters · case-insensitive)
**Hidden URLs:** `/dossier` (puzzle step 2), `/easteregg` (entry point)
**Prize phone (texts):** `2036091555`

## Step 1 · Notice the puzzle exists

The seed is planted in the [45-second hosts-intro video](https://quiz.miaswebsites.art/easteregg):

- **0–2s · Cold open.** The case folder slams down with a stamp that
  says **`ACCESS: _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _`** — eight dashes. That tells the
  viewer the final code is 8 characters.

The eight dashes are the only "this is a puzzle" tell. Everything
else looks like normal video chrome.

## Step 2 · Find the URL clue

- **6–10s · DOSSIER REVEAL.** The case folder zooms in tight. A small
  paper sticker on the spine reads:

  ```
  FILE://miaswebsites.art/dossier
  ```

  The sticker is rotated 90° (like a real binder spine label), in
  Courier monospace, ~18px on a 1920×1080 frame. It's readable but
  easy to miss in motion. A solver who pauses or rewinds the zoom
  beat will catch it.

  Direct link for your reference: <a href="https://quiz.miaswebsites.art/dossier">quiz.miaswebsites.art/dossier</a>.

## Step 3 · Visit /dossier

The dossier page is styled as an opened manilla case folder with a
"CLASSIFIED" watermark. The transcript inside is mostly redacted but
the visible fragments do two things:

1. **Misdirection.** "Hidden in plain sight," "small, yellow,
   friendly," "the daylight fell" — these are vibes-only clues. They
   confirm the theme (mascot, sun) but don't give the answer.
2. **The shift parameter.** One sentence reads in full:

   > "The code is built like a **cipher**: shifted forward by the
   > same number of **finalists** the broadcast announced. Refer to
   > the **video**. The number is printed in coral."

   That tells them: **the shift number = the number printed in coral
   in the broadcast**. The video has exactly one stat printed in
   coral instead of yellow — **"4 FINALISTS"** at 10–14s. So shift = 4.

3. **The cipher itself**, in a dashed box at the bottom of the page:

   ```
   XLI ERWAIV MW WYRWLMRI
   ```

   Labeled "CIPHER · CAESAR · SHIFT BACKWARD".

## Step 5 · Submit the code

The solver returns to <a href="https://quiz.miaswebsites.art/easteregg">/easteregg</a>
and enters `SUNSHINE` (case-insensitive, hyphens / spaces stripped).
The server-side action checks the answer and redirects to a success
state with:

- A "🌞 Access granted · You found the sunshine" lockup
- A **SESSION 001 · DECRYPTED** badge
- A big tappable `sms:2036091555` button — opens the Messages app on
  mobile
- A unique per-solve **SIG** (timestamp + random 3-char suffix) so
  every successful screenshot is verifiably different

## How to verify a screenshot is real

When someone texts you a screenshot, check three things:

1. **SESSION 001 · DECRYPTED** badge present, exact wording.
2. **SIG line** at the bottom, format `SIG · <base36-timestamp>·<3-uppercase-chars>`.
   The base36 timestamp decodes via `parseInt(part, 36)` and should
   be a recent Unix millisecond timestamp.
3. **🌞 mascot wink** at the bottom — easy to fake, but the SIG is
   the real proof.

If the SIG is missing or malformed, it's a fake.

## What to do if someone gets stuck

The order of partial hints, easiest to hardest:

1. "Did you watch the video on /easteregg?"
2. "Did you see the eight dashes on the file?"
3. "There's a URL hidden on the folder — try scrubbing the part
   where it zooms in."
4. "Once you find the URL, what does the page say about how to
   decode the line at the bottom?"
5. "The shift is in the video. It's the number printed in coral
   instead of yellow."
6. "Caesar cipher, shift backward by 4."
7. "The answer is the mascot's name. Picture the sun."

After (7), if they still need it, just tell them `SUNSHINE`.

Change the answer in **three** places (keep them in sync):

1. `app/easteregg/actions.ts` → `const ACCESS_CODE = "..."`
2. `app/dossier/page.tsx` → encrypt the new plaintext "THE ANSWER IS <NEW>"
   with the new Caesar shift, set as `CIPHER`

   printed in coral (or change the dossier text describing how to
