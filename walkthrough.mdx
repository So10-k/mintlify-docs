## How to verify a real screenshot

The success page renders a unique **SIG** per solve вЂ” `base36(now()) В· 3-uppercase-chars`. Check the SIG when someone texts you a screenshot:

- The base36 part decodes via `parseInt(sig.split("В·")[0], 36)` and should land in a recent millisecond range.
- The 3-character suffix should be uppercase alphanumeric.

If the SIG is missing or malformed, the screenshot is fake or doctored.
