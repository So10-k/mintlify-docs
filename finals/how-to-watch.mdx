The Grand Final video lands in your inbox when the edit is done. There's no scheduled time slot to be available for, no link to register for, and no platform to download — just a link you click whenever you want to watch.

## What you'll get and when

| | |
|---|---|
| **Where** | The email address you registered with at [/join](/joining). If it's changed, [email Sam](mailto:appdev7710@gmail.com) before the video ships. |
| **When** | Aiming for **Saturday May 23, 2026**. Sam will email a new ETA if it slips. |
| **Subject** | Watch your inbox for something like "🏆 The Grand Final is here — watch the video". The official subject is set right before send-out. |
| **Format** | A regular web video link. Open it in any browser, no app or login needed. |
| **Length** | Around 60–75 minutes for the full edit. You can pause, rewind, and rewatch as much as you want. |

<Note>
  The video is **public-link only** — no Zoho registration, no password, no "join the webinar" page. If the link works on your phone, it works on the TV in your living room too.
</Note>

## Three rules to keep the surprise

The pre-taped format means **the result is decided before you watch it**. To keep finals night magical for everyone, please:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Don't peek at the bracket until you've watched.">
    The [bracket](https://quiz.miaswebsites.art/standings) updates the moment the host runs the recording sessions, so the bracket WILL show the winner before the email goes out. If you want to stay surprised, skip the standings page until after you've watched the video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mute the forum's finals threads.">
    Hot takes start the second the video drops. The [Tournament Talk](https://discuss.miaswebsites.art/c/tournament-talk) category will have spoilers; mute the topic or wait to read it until after.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't text the finalists.">
    Two of the finalists already know the outcome (they played). Don't make them lie to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After you watch

- **Drop your reactions** in the [Tournament Talk](https://discuss.miaswebsites.art/c/tournament-talk) forum — the post-show recap thread is the place for hot takes, complaints, and theories about the championship questions you would or would not have gotten right.
- **Pick your bracket if you haven't.** Predictions close when the video drops, but the [predictions leaderboard](/tournament/predictions) is worth a look for who called it correctly.
- **Re-watch the championship round.** It's intentionally the hardest section — misc trivia, hard difficulty, every question on a different subject. You'll get more out of a second pass than you do the first time.

## What if I never got the email?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check spam, then check the right inbox.">
    The email goes out from `noreply@miaswebsites.art` through Mia's own provider (Resend or Brevo). Both have good deliverability, but a particularly aggressive spam filter will occasionally swallow them. The address used is the one you put in at [/join](/joining) — make sure that inbox is the one you're searching.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Sam.">
    If it really isn't there after the ETA, [email Sam](mailto:appdev7710@gmail.com). He has the send log on `/host/email-analytics` and can confirm whether the message left, whether it bounced, and resend if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bookmark the bracket page as backup.">
    Even if the email goes sideways, the [standings page](https://quiz.miaswebsites.art/standings) will show the final result. Not as fun as watching the video, but at least you'll know who won.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
