Mia's Quiz Tournament is a weekly quiz competition created and hosted by Mia, age 8. Each week a new round of quiz questions drops, and your goal is simple: answer enough questions correctly to stay in the game. Players who pass each round keep going; players who don't collect a strike. Collect too many strikes and you're eliminated. The last player standing wins the grand prize — **hang out with Mia!**

## How the tournament works

The tournament moves through three phases from start to finish:

**Sign-ups open** — Registration is open and you can join at `/join`. Once Mia closes sign-ups and starts the first round, no new players can enter.

**Rounds running** — A new quiz round drops each week. Head to `/play` before time runs out, answer every question, and hit the pass threshold to keep all your strikes where they are. Miss the deadline or fall below the threshold and you earn a strike. Earn too many strikes and you're eliminated.

**Tournament complete** — When Mia ends the tournament, the champion is announced and wins the prize.

<Note>
  You must sign up before registration closes. Once the first round starts, the doors are shut and no new players can join.
</Note>

## Tournament phases at a glance

| Phase | What's happening | What you should do |
|---|---|---|
| Sign-ups open | Registration is accepting new players | Go to `/join` and sign up |
| Rounds running | Weekly quiz rounds are live | Go to `/play` before each round closes |
| Tournament complete | A champion has been crowned | Check `/standings` to see the final results |

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on the home page — it shows the current tournament status, a countdown to the next round, and a big button that takes you exactly where you need to go.
</Tip>
