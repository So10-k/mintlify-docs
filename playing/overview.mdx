The `/play` page is your home base for the tournament. Everything you need during a competition lives here: your current lives, this week's active round, practice rounds, and a log of every past round you've played. As soon as you sign in, you land here.

## Status banner

At the top of the page you'll see a banner with your name and your current lives. Each heart icon represents one life you still have. When you lose a life, the filled heart becomes an empty one. If you've been eliminated, the banner shows "Out of the game" instead of hearts.

<Info>
  The number of hearts you start with is set by the host. Most tournaments begin with **2 hearts**.
</Info>

## Current round card

Below the banner is the main card for this week's round. What you see there depends on your situation:

- **Round open, not yet played** — a "Play this round!" button links directly to the round.
- **Round open, already submitted** — a "See your answers" button lets you review what you submitted.
- **Eliminated** — a "Cheer the others on" link takes you to the players page at `/players`.
- **Waiting for next round** — the host is writing the next round and hasn't opened it yet.
- **Tournament over** — a link to `/standings` so you can see who won.

## Practice rounds section

If any practice rounds are available, they appear in a card below the main round. Practice rounds are always open and never affect your standing or lives — they exist purely for fun and warm-up. Each entry shows the round title, your score if you've already completed it, and a button to play or review it.

<Tip>
  Practice rounds are available to every signed-in player, including players who have been eliminated from the main tournament.
</Tip>

## Past rounds list

Below the practice section you'll find a list of every closed round from the tournament so far. Each entry shows the round number, its title, your score as a percentage, and a link to review your answers. If you missed a round entirely, the entry shows "missed" instead of a score.
