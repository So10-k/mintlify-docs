Each round of the tournament is a quiz with multiple-choice or true/false questions. The host writes the round, opens it at a set time, and closes it when the window ends. Your job is to play before it closes and answer enough questions correctly to meet the pass threshold.

## Round lifecycle

Every round moves through three states:

- **Draft** — the round has been written but not yet opened. You can't see or play draft rounds.
- **Active** — the round is open and playable. The current round card on `/play` links directly to it.
- **Closed** — the round has ended. You can review your answers but can't change them.

<Note>
  Only one real tournament round can be active at a time. The host opens and closes rounds manually.
</Note>

## How to play an active round

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to /play">
    The main card on your dashboard shows the active round with its title and a short introduction. Click **Play this round!** to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the intro">
    Each round starts with an optional intro passage. Read it carefully — questions are often based on the content it describes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer each question">
    Work through the questions one by one. Each question is either multiple-choice (pick one answer from several options) or true/false.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your answers">
    When you've answered every question, submit. You can only submit once per round, so review your picks before confirming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scoring and the pass threshold

Your score is the number of correct answers divided by the total number of questions. For example, if a round has 10 questions and you get 7 right, your score is **70%**.

To pass a round you need to meet the pass threshold, which is set by the host for each round. The default threshold is **60%** — meaning you need to answer at least 60% of questions correctly.

| Your score | Threshold | Result |
|---|---|---|
| 70% | 60% | Pass — no heart lost |
| 50% | 60% | Fail — lose 1 heart |
| 100% | 60% | Pass — no heart lost |

<Info>
  The pass threshold can vary from round to round. It's always shown on the round page before you submit.
</Info>

## After you submit

Once you submit, you're taken directly to your answer review. You can see every question, the option you chose, and whether it was correct. Your final score is shown as a percentage at the top.

You can return to review your answers any time by going to `/play` and clicking the "See your answers" link for a past or the current round.

## If you miss a round

If a round closes before you submit, the system automatically records a score of **0%** for you. A score of 0% doesn't meet any pass threshold, so you lose a heart automatically when the round closes. Missing a round has the same effect as failing one.

<Warning>
  Don't wait until the last minute. If the round closes while you still have the page open, your answers won't be accepted and you'll receive a score of 0.
</Warning>
