Welcome to **Mia's Quiz Tournament** — a fun, friendly quiz competition where you answer questions each week, collect hearts, and try to outlast the competition. Sign up with just your name and email — sign-in runs through **MiaAuth**, our email-only auth flow (powered by Auth0 by Okta on the front door, and Auth.js underneath), so there's no password to manage. Every week a new quiz round drops; get enough answers right to stay in the game.

## How the tournament works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to **/join**, enter your name and email, and click the one-time magic link in your inbox. The link signs you in, creates your account, and enrolls you in the tournament.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in next time">
    Returning? Go to **/signin** and tap **Sign in with MiaAuth**. Auth0 sends you a 6-digit code, you enter it, you're back in. No password ever.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play each round">
    When a round opens, head to **/play** and answer the quiz questions before time runs out. Each round has a pass threshold — clear it to keep all your hearts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Survive elimination">
    You start with a set number of hearts (lives). Miss a round or fail to pass the threshold and you lose a heart. Lose them all and you're out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Win the prize">
    The last player standing — or the one with the highest score when the host ends the tournament — is crowned champion and wins the prize!
  </Step>
</Steps>
