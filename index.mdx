## How a season runs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up at /join" icon="ticket">
    Tell us your name and email. We send a one-time magic link to your
    inbox. Click it once and you&rsquo;re in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign back in at /signin" icon="key">
    Returning? Tap **Sign in with MiaAuth** — a 6-digit code lands in
    your inbox. No password ever.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play each round at /play" icon="gamepad-2">
    A new quiz round drops every week. Answer before the timer ends.
    Clear the pass threshold and your hearts are safe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Survive the bracket" icon="medal">
    You start with two hearts. Miss a round or fail the threshold and
    you lose one. Lose both and the bracket spits you into the losers&rsquo;
    side; lose again and you&rsquo;re out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Grand Final" icon="crown">
    Four finalists. Two bracket finals + a championship. One champion
    at the end — and an end-credits slide with their name on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Grand Final this season

The Grand Final is **pre-taped** for Season 1. Mia and Sam record each
match one-on-one, edit them together into a watch-anytime video, and
email the link to every enrolled player. See [The Finals overview](/finals/overview)
for the four finalists and the format, or jump straight to [How to
watch](/finals/how-to-watch) for what to expect when the email lands.
