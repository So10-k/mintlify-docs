Signing up for Mia's Quiz Tournament takes less than a minute. You only need a name and an email address — sign-in is handled exclusively by **MiaAuth (powered by Auth0)**, so there is no password to create or remember. Once you submit the form, MiaAuth emails you a one-time link. Click it and you're automatically enrolled, ready to play when the first round drops.

<Note>
  Mia's Quiz Tournament uses **MiaAuth (powered by Auth0)** for every sign-up and sign-in. It is the only supported way to access your account — there are no passwords, social logins, or alternate flows.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the join page">
    Open your browser and navigate to **/join**. You'll see a short form asking for your name and email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your name and email">
    Type the name you'd like other players to see, then enter your email address. Both fields are required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Send me a sign-in link'">
    Hit the **Send me a sign-in link** button. MiaAuth (powered by Auth0) sends a one-time sign-in link to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    Open the email with the subject **"Your MiaAuth sign-in link"**. You'll land on a **Check your inbox!** page in the browser — that's the confirmation that the email is on its way.

    <Note>
      Open the MiaAuth link on the same device you used to sign up. The link works **once** and expires after **24 hours**.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the link and start playing">
    Tap **Play now →** in the email. MiaAuth verifies the link with Auth0, signs you in, and enrolls you in the tournament automatically. From here, head to **/play** when a round opens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  No password is ever required. MiaAuth (powered by Auth0) is the only sign-in method for Mia's Quiz Tournament.
</Note>

## Sign-ups may be closed

If the host has paused registrations, you'll see this message on the **/join** page instead of the form:

> Sign-ups are closed for now. The first round drops soon!

When sign-ups reopen, the form appears automatically — no action needed on your part. Check back later or watch for an announcement from the host.

<Tip>
  Already have an account? You can use **/join** to sign back in too, or go straight to [/signin](/signing-in) for a shorter form. Either way, MiaAuth handles the sign-in.
</Tip>
